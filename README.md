# HomeMap

Point your phone at any room and watch it build a precise 3D model in minutes.

HomeMap connects that scan directly to platforms like TaskRabbit, Handy, and Thumbtack so organizers, designers, and contractors arrive already knowing your space. No tape measures, no blurry photos, no wasted first-hour walkthroughs. The professional you book shows up with your floor plan already loaded and an AI-generated organization plan ready to execute.

## The problem

The home services market is $600B and almost entirely booked blind. Contractors quote without measuring, organizers arrive without knowing the room, designers work from blurry phone photos. HomeMap changes what a service can promise before anyone shows up.

## How it works

1. Scan any room with your phone
2. HomeMap builds a precise 3D model
3. Share the model when you book any home service
4. Every pro you hire inherits your full space context

## Model

Direct integration with TaskRabbit and Thumbtack as the launch channel. Subscription for homeowners, referral fee from service marketplace integrations. Space optimization AI as the premium tier.
